\b;\button 170; Nuclear Plant
\image nuclear 8 8;
The nuclear plant is a \l;nuclear power cell\u object\atomic; factory. It transforms a chunk of \l;uranium ore\u object\uranore; into a \l;nuclear power cell\u object\atomic; fully charged and ready for use.

\t;Requirements for building
Any flat surface of at least 10 meters wide.

\t;Input
Uranium ore.

\t;Output
Nuclear power cell.

\t;Power source
None.

\t;Duration of operation
30 seconds.
