\b;Mine
\image mine 8 8;
Mines were a defensive weapons system used by the first expedition. Because of the dangers this system causes for the mission itself, they are no longer used in your expedition. In some exercises mines are used to learn how to avoid obstacles. There is no way to remove them or to create new ones.

With a \l;practice bot\u object\bottr;, don't get any closer than 2 meters to the position given by the instruction \l;radar\u cbot\radar;.
