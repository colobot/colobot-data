\b;Organic Matter
\image bullet 4 4;
The organic matter is the insects' secretion. \l;Wasps\u object\wasp; often use balls of organic matter to bombard you. You should perhaps try to get hold of some since it could allow you, given the necessary \l;analysis\u object\labo;, to discover new and strange technologies that will improve the efficiency of your bots.
