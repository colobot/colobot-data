\b;\button 175; Vault
\image safe 8 8;
This dome-shaped building was conceived by the first expedition. Its purpose was to offer protection to a new type of advanced bot. To protect it from insect attacks, the vault was locked by 4 \l;keys\u object\key;. Only after you find the 4 keys and place each one of them on their corresponding slots will you be given access to the bot.
