\b;Spider
\image spider 8 8;
Spiders work like suicide commandoes: whenever a spider gets within a close enough range of its target, it inflates its abdomen and bursts into a multitude of tiny burning fragments. These fragments will set fire to everything they get in contact with. Luckily the spider won't survive.
