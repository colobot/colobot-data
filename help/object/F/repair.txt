\b;\button 169; Repair Center
\image repair 8 8;
The repair center regenerates the shield on damaged bots. A bot's shield will absorb a certain amount of \l;enemy\u object\mother; hits. If there is no shield left, this means that the bot itself, when hit next, will be destroyed.

\t;Requirements for building
Any flat surface of at least 6 meters wide.

\t;Input
Damaged bot.

\t;Output
Repaired bot with regenerated shield.

\t;Power source
None.

\t;Duration of operation
A few seconds depending on the repair job to be done.

\c;RepairCenter\n;
